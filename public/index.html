Half-Life

Half-Life is a legendary first-person shooter game developed by Valve Corporation. It was first released in 1998 and quickly became a landmark title in the gaming industry.

The game follows the adventures of Dr. Gordon Freeman, a theoretical physicist who must fight his way through a research facility overrun by alien creatures and government soldiers.

With its gripping storyline, immersive atmosphere, and innovative gameplay, Half-Life set new standards for the first-person shooter genre and has since spawned numerous sequels and spin-offs.
